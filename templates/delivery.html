Shipping & Handling


We ship orders to the following U.S. destinations: Continental U.S., Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, Guam and military addresses (APO and FPO). To assure quick, reliable delivery, we ship all orders to non-P.O. box addresses within the continental U.S. via FedEx Ground. Some orders may be delivered by FedEx to your local post office for final delivery to your door. These orders can still be tracked through FedEx. Orders shipped to P.O. box addresses within the continental U.S., as well as orders outside of the continental U.S., are shipped via USPS Priority Mail. This includes orders shipped to Alaska, Hawaii, Guam, Puerto Rico and military addresses (APO and FPO.) Shipping charges are determined by your order's subtotal amount (less any eGift Certificates purchased).
